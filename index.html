Our Mission

We are a student society aimed at new, intermediate and advanced student programmers and our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and industry, creating leaders that will advance the future of global technology.
We are a diverse community of software developers and technology enthusiasts who are committed to contributing significantly to the developement community at UCT. We work with companies and organizations to help our members grow their skills in software development and hold various events aimed at helping our members grow in the field of software development.
